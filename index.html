The Duck Behind the Seal

Made by Hand, for People Who Notice

I collect stamps and I press wax. Every piece in the gallery is made by hand: framed sets, loose impressions, and little magnet keepsakes, each one its own small world in wax.

It's wax seal art for the curious, the collectors, and anyone who finds joy in correspondence that feels like it means something. Now and then a duck even slips away to sea, but mostly, the work is made to be kept.

A Small Tradition

A Duck at Sea

Now and then a seal goes travelling. When I sail, I bring a few handmade duck magnets aboard and leave them for strangers to find. It's my small part in the Cruising Ducks tradition: each one tucked beside a card that reads what luck, you've found a duck.
