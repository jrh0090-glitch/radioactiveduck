The Duck Behind the Seal

Made by Hand, Made to Be Broken

Radioactive Duck is one person, a drawer full of stamp heads, and twenty years of pressed wax. I've been collecting wax seals my whole adult life. Somewhere along the way the collection started producing things: framed sets, loose impressions, little magnet keepsakes, seals for other people's mail.

This is a hobby, not a livelihood, and I keep it small on purpose. I press to order, I pack carefully, and I like being paid for my work, but no duck is keeping the lights on here.

A Duck at Sea

Now and then a seal goes travelling. When I sail, I bring a few handmade magnets aboard and leave them for strangers to find. Just my little part in the Cruising Ducks tradition.
